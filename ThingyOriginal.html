Overview

Conservation International is a non-profit environmental organization that has been protecting nature for 25 years. Conservation International sees themselves more as a human organization than an environmental organization since human beings are dependent on nature and when saving nature we are really trying to save ourselves. CI is more than 1,000 people in 30+ countries helping to build a healthier, more prosperous and more productive planet for you and everyone. This organization has many projects all around the world with different issues such as Climate, Food, Fresh Water, Global Stability, Ocean etc.

Nature Is Speaking Campaigne

Nature Is Speaking campaign is a film series by Conservation International which features 8 short films that focus on the slogan of CI which is "Nature Does Not Need people. People Need Nature." These short films covers the topic of: Mother Nature, The Ocean, The Rainforest, The Soil, Water, The Redwood, Coral Reef and Flower. All the films are narrated by famous Actors such as Julia Robert, Kevin Spacey, Ian Somerhalder, Lupita Nyong'o ect... These short films surrounds the question of if nature will evolving with or without us and how every human being needs to come together and make sure the answer to that question is with us. This series of films gives the viewer a better understanding on how all aspects of nature are needed for us in order to survive. Below is 2 out of 8 Campaign films.
