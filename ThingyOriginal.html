Intoduction

This website is made by four beginner coding students that learned how to code in less than a month. We have come together with a common goal this summer to learn how to code and raise awareness of global warming in order to live a healthier and more productive life for everyone. We carefully picked an organization called 'Conservation International' which has the same goal as we do. With this organization we hope to raise money to support a cause to protect the most essential things that the earth offers us. All the donation we recieve will go towards a good cause on perserving the earth we live on.

Overview

Conservation International is a non-profit environmental organization that has been protecting nature for 25 years. Conservation International sees themselves more as a human organization than an environmental organization since human beings are dependent on nature and when saving nature we are really trying to save ourselves. CI is more than 1,000 people in 30+ countries helping to build a healthier, more prosperous and more productive planet for you and everyone. This organization has many projects all around the world with different issues such as Climate, Food, Fresh Water, Global Stability, Ocean etc.

Vision

"We imagine a healthy, prosperous world in which societies are forever committed to caring for and valuing nature, for the long-term benefit of people and all life on Earth."-Conservation International

Mission

"Building upon a strong foundation of science, partnership and field demonstration, CI empowers societies to responsibly and sustainably care for nature, our global biodiversity, for the well-being of humanity."- Consercation International
